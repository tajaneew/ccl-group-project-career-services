CCL Careers

Color Coded Labs Careers is an university- wide initiative designed to support students throughout their professional development, with a focus on connecting them with local, national, and global organizations.

Why Choose Us

Individualized Success Plan

Every CCL student receives a personalized Career Success plan. Your plan will give you career-building activities at every stage of your Color Coded Labs journey, ensuring that you’re ready for a great job as soon as you graduate.

“A solid plan helps you manage your course schedule, graduate faster, and launch your career. At Career Services, we provide personalized, one-on-one career counseling to our students, as well as a variety of online tools to help you explore potential careers. We also help connect students with job shadowing and work-study programs.”

Why Choose Us

CCL offers workshops on the following topics:

  • Interview Preparation
  • Resume and Cover Letter Writing
  • Networking
  • Proper Business Etiquette
  • Job Searching
  • Salary Negotiation
  • Career Exploration and Coaching
  • Employer Relations

Why Career Services

Passion. Purpose. Calling.

We deliver innovative career experiences through engaging in experiential learning activities that prepare students for the workplace, by providing best-in-class career education, interactive workshops, and by building strategic employer partnerships.

Jumpstart Your Career With Handshake.

CCL utilizes Handshake, our university- wide position posting system where students have access to search and apply for part-time, internships, co-ops, and full time career opportunities. Opportunities are posted as well as information on upcoming career fairs and on-campus recruiting events.
